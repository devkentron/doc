## Overview

Global compliance, privacy, and cybersecurity regulations significantly impact how organizations protect and manage their communications data. 

Kentron's AI-powered Predicted Compliance Engine proactively identifies domain-specific requirements of various regulatory frameworks, enabling the identification of risks across dynamic video, audio, and text communications.
