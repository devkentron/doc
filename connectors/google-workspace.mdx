---
title: Google Workspace
---

Capture critical data seamlessly from your business's Google Workspace accounts, including Gmail, Google Drive, and Google Vault.

Kentron's Unified Injection Engine supports collecting the following data:

- Gmail 
- Google Drive 
- Shared Drive 
- Google Vault
- Deleted emails
- Deleted fields from Google Drive 
- Attachments 
- Labels
- Discarded draft email
