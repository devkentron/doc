Capture critical email data from your Gmail accounts.

## Data Collection Capabilities

Kentron's Unified Injection Engine supports collecting the following data:

### Email Content
- Inbox messages
- Sent messages
- Draft emails
- Deleted emails
- Email attachments

### Organization
- Labels and categories
- Filters and rules
- Priority inbox settings

### Advanced Features
- Email threading
- Conversation view data
- Spam and trash content
