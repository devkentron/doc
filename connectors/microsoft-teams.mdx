
Capture critical communication data from Microsoft Teams.

## Data Collection Capabilities

Kentron's Unified Injection Engine supports collecting the following data:

### Communication Data
- Channel messages
- Private chats
- Meeting conversations
- Voice and video call logs

### Content
- Shared files
- Attachments
- Links
- Reactions and emojis

### Team Structure
- Team membership
- Channel structure
- Permissions and roles
