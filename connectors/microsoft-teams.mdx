title: Microsoft Teams
---

Capture critical data seamlessly from Microsoft Teams. Analyze communications and unlock powerful insights with on-demand data collection.

Kentron's Unified Injection Engine supports collecting the following data:

- Channel messages
- Private chats
- Meeting conversations
- Voice and video call logs
- Shared files
- Attachments
- Links
- Reactions and emojis
- Team membership
- Channel structure
- Permissions and roles
