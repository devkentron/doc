
Capture critical email and calendar data from Microsoft Outlook.

## Data Collection Capabilities

Kentron's Unified Injection Engine supports collecting the following data:

### Email Data
- Inbox messages
- Sent items
- Deleted emails
- Draft messages
- Email attachments

### Calendar Data
- Appointments
- Meeting invites
- Calendar sharing
- Event attachments

### Organization
- Folders structure
- Categories and labels
- Rules and filters
